speak to someone

online:

  • Just need to speak to someone?
  • Better Help (theraoy;not covered by insurance)
  • Talk Space (therapy;not covered by insurance)

by phone:

  • 1-800-273-8255 (National Suicide Prevention Lifeline)
  • 1-877-726-4727 (SAMHSA (Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration))

in person:

  • Speak to a friend; maybe one you haven't talked to in a while.
  • Talk to a trusted family member.
  • Ask a teacher or a mentor you confide in for help.
  • Consider speaking to your doctor or school counselor for help setting up an appointment with a therapist.
  • Find a therapist online. There are many resources, like Google; Breakthrough is also dedicated to helping find therapists.
